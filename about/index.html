My Name is Jack Warfield and I am a graduate student studying astronomy at the University of Virginia.

I am originally from Fulton County, Ohio, and graduated from The Ohio State University with a B.S. in astronomy and physics with honors research distinction in astronomy, where I worked with Marc Pinsonneault, Jennifer Johnson, and Joel Zinn studying red giants observed by K2 and APOGEE. I am now at the University of Virginia working with Nitya Kallivayalil studying near-field cosmology, focusing on finding PM for and characterizing dwarf galaxies around M31.
